Design Bias

Prologue

Design Bias is an interactive parallax website. It aims to use parallax web design for the storytelling and education of implicit AI bias that affects millennials & gen z age groups. Design Bias aims To inspire future designers and users to think about their technology with an acute awareness of their effects. “Unfortunately, we have biases that live in our data, and if we don’t acknowledge that and if we don’t take specific actions to address it then we’re just going to continue to perpetuate them or even make them worse.” — Kathy Baxter, Ethical AI Practice Architect, Salesforce.
